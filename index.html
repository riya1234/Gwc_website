Welcome to Seed Data Hub

Our consultants at Seed Data Hub are solving our clients' most pressing environmental issues utilizing available data and developing technology - whether it be via a mobile/web application, machine learning/statistical model, Application Programming Interface, or anything within our capabilities to solve these issues.

More than solving our clients' issues, Seed Data Hub aggregates data and centralizes it for future use by any third party via a RESTful API. We hope this way there can be a central entry point for all the valuable data avaialble in the environmental space. With our partners, we strive to make environmental concerns central and increase transperancy of all data resources for those concerned.

We hope by aggregating and analyzing data and open sourcing our analytics through readily-accessible forms of media such as mobile, web, tablet, etc we may help in making everyone a little more aware of the environmental issues confronting us today.
